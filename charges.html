Our Charges

Accommodation Assistance

We charge a flat fee of ₹2,000 for our accommodation assistance services. This fee includes:

  • Assistance in finding suitable accommodation options
  • Guidance on the rental process
  • Support throughout your stay

Visa Assistance

Our visa assistance services are priced at $Y per application. This fee covers:

  • Preparation and review of visa application documents
  • Guidance on visa interview preparation (if applicable)
  • Support throughout the visa application process

Statement of Purpose (SOP) Help

We offer SOP help at a rate of ₹5,000 per document. This fee includes:

  • Personalized guidance in crafting a compelling SOP
  • Review and editing services
  • Unlimited revisions until you're satisfied

English Language Requirement Courses

We provide preparation courses for various English language proficiency exams:

  • IELTS (International English Language Testing System): Course fee varies depending on the duration and intensity of the course.
  • Duolingo English Test: Course fee varies depending on the package chosen.
  • PTE (Pearson Test of English): Course fee varies depending on the course format and duration.
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examination): Course fee varies depending on the duration and level of preparation required.
